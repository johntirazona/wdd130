Philippines

The Philippines is a Southeast Asian country made up of over 7,000 islands, known for its stunning beaches, rich biodiversity, and vibrant culture. It has a tropical climate and is home to natural wonders like the Banaue Rice Terraces, Taal Volcano, and the underground river in Palawan. The Filipino people are known for their warmth, hospitality, and strong sense of community. With a blend of indigenous, Spanish, American, and Asian influences, the Philippines offers a unique cultural and historical experience.
