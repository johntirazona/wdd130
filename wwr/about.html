Our History

Down The River Rafting began with a simple dream: to share the thrill of whitewater with the world. Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate river guides, our company started with a single raft and a deep love for nature. Over the years, we’ve grown into one of the most trusted names in adventure tourism, known for safety, unforgettable experiences, and a true sense of camaraderie.

From humble beginnings on local rivers, we’ve expanded to include some of the most iconic rapids in the region. We’ve guided thousands of guests — from first-time paddlers to seasoned adrenaline junkies — helping each one create lasting memories in the heart of the wild. Our commitment to excellence, respect for the environment, and passion for adventure continue to drive us forward.
